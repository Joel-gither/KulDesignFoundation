Various activities that youth Engage in daily

Spiritual Growth

Through Jinja Salvation Church to nature spiritual well-being and community growth.

Child spiritual growth is a journey of developing a relationship with the divine and understanding spiritual concepts in a way that is relevant to their age and experiences. It involves nurturing their innate sense of wonder, teaching them about faith, values, and ethics, and providing opportunities for them to connect with something larger than themselves. This can be fostered through storytelling, prayer, rituals, nature exploration, and acts of kindness, helping them build a foundation for a meaningful and compassionate life.

Vocational Skills

Children and Youth Engage in skill development.

Youth vocational skills training equips young individuals with practical skills and knowledge necessary for specific trades or occupations. This type of training focuses on hands-on learning and prepares youth for direct entry into the workforce, self-employment, or further technical education. It plays a crucial role in addressing youth unemployment, fostering economic independence, and meeting the demands of various industries. Examples of vocational skills training include carpentry, tailoring, automotive repair, information technology, hospitality, and agriculture.

Youth Training Workshop

Equips youth entrepreneurship skills and knowledge to boost their confidence.

Youth training workshops are focused, short-term programs designed to equip young people with specific skills, knowledge, or attitudes relevant to personal and professional development. These interactive sessions often cover topics like leadership, communication, entrepreneurship, digital literacy, problem-solving, and career readiness. By providing practical tools and fostering active participation, workshops empower youth to enhance their capabilities, boost their confidence, and navigate future challenges effectively.

Scholarship program

Enables children and youth attain academic advancement in the community.

Scholarship programs for youth and children provide financial assistance to enable access to education and skills development opportunities. These programs aim to alleviate financial barriers, allowing young individuals to pursue their academic or vocational aspirations. Scholarships can cover tuition fees, learning materials, accommodation, and other related expenses, fostering educational equity and empowering the next generation. They often target talented students from underprivileged backgrounds, specific fields of study, or those demonstrating leadership potential or community involvement.

Clean Water

Improves health and sanitation by securing and maintaining clean water sources.

Advocating for clean water is crucial for public health, environmental sustainability, and human dignity. It involves raising awareness about water scarcity and pollution, lobbying for policies that protect water resources, and supporting initiatives that provide access to safe and affordable drinking water and sanitation. This advocacy is essential for preventing waterborne diseases, preserving ecosystems, and ensuring a healthy future for all communities, particularly vulnerable populations.

Sustainable Agriculture

Promote farming development initiatives that enhance food security.

Sustainable agriculture focuses on producing food, fiber, and other agricultural products using methods that protect the environment, public health, and animal welfare. It aims to meet present and future societal needs without depleting natural resources. Key principles include minimizing soil degradation, reducing water pollution, conserving biodiversity, using resources efficiently, and promoting economic viability for farmers. Sustainable practices often involve crop rotation, integrated pest management, agroforestry, and water-wise irrigation.

Who we are

Get to know about KDYFO

The overall goal of KDYFO is to develop Health, Skilled, Responsible Citizen sustainable economic prgress

KDYFO was founded in 2000 in Kibibi Budondo with a clear mission to empower vulnerable children through education, to provide access to clean water in underserved communities, to support families affected by poverty. Recognizing a critical gap in Education, a group of passionate individuals came together to create a sustainable and impactful solution. Initially starting with clean water installation, the organization has grown to several schools while remaining committed to its core values of compassion, integrity, empowerment.
